Cloud Kitchen Management


Managed end to end tech platforms for cloud kitchens of all capacities, right from restaurant onboarding to last mile delivery.







As consumers shift their dining behaviour increasingly to delivery, it’s impossible for restaurants to ignore the large appetite for this new model. Even industry trends have started showing up in favour. According to a recent survey, 67% of the restaurant owners would want to open a cloud kitchen as their next outlet.

This huge demand is being met at a faster and efficient manner with our end to end cloud kitchen software suite. Our technical expertise and automation helps businesses focus on their customers by concentrating on their food and expansion, instead of backend technical know how.


Our Services Include:

  • Restaurant Onboarding Software
  • Analytics engine for internal and external data
  • Peer to peer communication with restaurant owners
  • Interactive dashboard for decision makers
  • Real time tracking and geo-tagging engine
  • Last mile delivery tech stack
  • Customer outreach engine
  • Real time inventory management system
  • Internal HRMS Software
